Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first detail.

Do you come to my home or office?

Yes — we're fully mobile. We bring everything to your driveway, apartment lot, or office parking spot anywhere in Allen, TX and nearby areas. Just let us know where to park when you book.

Do you need access to water or electricity?

Yes — we'll need access to a water spigot and a standard power outlet at your location. Most homes have both readily available. If you're not sure whether your spot will work, just mention it when you book and we'll help you figure it out.

How long does a detail take?

It depends on the package and your vehicle's condition. As a rough guide: an Exterior Detail runs about 45–60 minutes, an Interior Detail about 60–75 minutes, and a Full Detail around 90–120 minutes. We'll give you a clearer estimate when you book.

What areas do you serve?

Allen, TX and the surrounding communities. If you're just outside the area, reach out anyway — we'll let you know if we can make it work.

How do I pay?

We accept just about everything — credit and debit card, cash, tap to pay, and Zelle. Card payments run securely through Square. Payment is due after the detail is complete and you're happy with the results.

What if my car is really dirty or has pet hair?

No problem — just mention it in the "anything else" box when you book so we can plan the right amount of time. Heavier jobs may take a little longer, and we'll always be upfront if anything affects pricing.

How do I book?

Use the booking form just below — pick a package, date, and time, and we'll confirm by phone or text within a few hours. Prefer to talk first? Call us at (945) 248-1256.
